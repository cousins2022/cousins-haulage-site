Chris Cousins

Owner Operator – Cousins Haulage Ltd

After leaving school and first qualifying as a vehicle mechanic Chris then took his place as a 3rd generation truck driver following in his father and grandfathers footsteps racking up over 20 years on artics before starting his own courier company in 2022 and he is just one half of the company as in 2023 his wife Natasha joined in to create the husband and wife team they are today. Chris says " Cousins Haulage Ltd is dedicated to maintaining it's reputation for reliability on every trip we undertake . We want our customers to load their product onto our vehicle and not have to worry safe in the knowledge from that point on the job is as good as done to a standard they will appreciate."

Over the last 12 months the focus has been building the foundations up focusing on training to keep alignment with Cpc driver modules and undertaking the steps and training required to achieve Transport Manager Qualification then furthermore a successful aquistion of an O licence now enabling the company to grow into some bigger vehicles moving forward. Cousins Haulage ltd has now taken delivery of a purpose built 3.5t Renault Master which fully complies with ATP/FRC European legislation enabling us to undertake journeys across Europe and beyond.
